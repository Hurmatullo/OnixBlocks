1. Data Collection

The Onix Blocks application does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data from users. The application does not request names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other information that could be used to identify a user.

The application may store the following data locally on the user's device:

3. Third-Party Services

The application does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or marketing services and does not share data with third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data stored on the user's device. The application does not access, upload, or remotely manage user data.
